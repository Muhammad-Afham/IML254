My Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy watching anime and Kamen Rider. I began watching anime and Kamen Rider while I was in elementary school.

Anime has a diverse range of genres, from fierce shonen combat in shows like "My Hero Academia" and "Attack on Titan" to emotional dramas like "Your Lie in April" and psychological thrillers like "Death Note." The rich world-building and inventive themes provide unique and interesting experiences.

Kamen Rider, a cornerstone of tokusatsu entertainment, is renowned for its heroic themes, lavish costumes, and thrilling fight choreography. From "Kamen Rider Ichigo" until now, each series introduces a new protagonist with unique skills and frequently delves into deeper moral or philosophical issues.

Many people believe that this anime and Kamen Rider are only suitable for kids. However, I believe that this narrative is suited for all age groups.
